# FSA Eligible Water Filter — fsaeligiblewaterfilter.com

A complete, static SEO site about when home water filtration qualifies as an HSA/FSA
medical expense (via a Letter of Medical Necessity), and how to buy it with pre-tax
dollars through SpringWell's TrueMed checkout.

**71 pages** · pillar + 58 supporting articles + reviews, comparisons, About Us,
Contact (with form), HTML sitemap, and full legal set. All HTML/CSS/JS — no build step
required to deploy. Author/founder: Stephen Evangelista.

---

## Publish in 3 steps (Vercel + GitHub)

1. **Push to GitHub.** Create a repo and commit the entire contents of this folder
   (index.html, all pages, /css, /js, /assets, vercel.json, robots.txt, sitemap.xml).
2. **Import to Vercel.** New Project → import the repo. No framework, no build command,
   output directory = root. Deploy.
3. **Add the domain.** In Vercel → Settings → Domains, add `fsaeligiblewaterfilter.com`
   and point your DNS as Vercel instructs.

To preview locally first: `python3 -m http.server` in this folder, then open
http://localhost:8000 (note: cloaked /go/ redirects and the contact form only work on
the live Vercel deployment).

## Regenerating the site

All pages are generated by `build.py`. To edit content, change `build.py` and run:

```
python3 build.py
```

This rewrites every .html plus sitemap.xml and vercel.json.

## How it's wired

- **Affiliate links are cloaked.** Real SpringWell tracking URLs (Everflow, affid=40)
  live ONLY in `vercel.json` as 14 `/go/{product}` redirects. No raw tracking URL
  appears in any HTML. `/go/` is disallowed in robots.txt.
- **cleanUrls** is on, so pages resolve without the `.html` extension (e.g. `/sitemap`).
- **SEO:** unique titles/descriptions, Article/FAQ/Review/Organization/Person schema,
  EPA/CDC/NSF/IRS citations, full internal linking, XML + HTML sitemaps.

## Two things to finish after deploy

1. **Activate the contact form.** It posts to FormSubmit.co. After deploy, submit the
   form once and click the confirmation email FormSubmit sends you. (To swap to your own
   handler/Formspree, change the `<form action>` in the Contact section of build.py.)
2. **Self-host the founder photo (optional).** The byline/About photo currently loads
   from an external URL. To self-host, save it as `assets/founder.png` and set
   `FOUNDER_IMG = "/assets/founder.png"` near the top of build.py, then rebuild.

## Notes

- Legal pages (Terms, Privacy, Cookie, Disclaimer, Affiliate Disclosure, Accessibility)
